Important Notice

If clicking the button fails to automatically open all links, your browser may be blocking pop-ups. Please follow these steps:

  • Chrome/Edge: Click the pop-up icon on the right side of the address bar → Select "Always allow pop-ups from this site"
  • Firefox: Click the lock icon on the left side of the address bar → Permissions → Allow pop-ups
  • Safari: Preferences → Websites → Pop-up Windows → Select "Allow"

Alternatively, you can manually click the links in the list above to open them one by one.
